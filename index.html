About

I'm a third-year PhD candidate in Economics at the Geneva Graduate Institute, supervised by Pr. Cajal-Grossi and Pr. Raisaro. My research interests are in development economics and trade.

Before the PhD, I worked as a Trade Information Specialist at the International Trade Centre (UNCTAD/WTO) and as a Research Assistant at the World Trade Organization. I hold an MSc in Economics from the Geneva Graduate Institute and a BSc in Economics from HEC Lausanne.

I speak French, Italian, English, and some Portuguese.
